Introduction

Story

In the ancient time of China, Chi You the leader of the monster tribe started a rebellion to overturn the ruling of Yellow Emperor. Yellow Emperor united his tribe with another powerful tribe leader, Yan Emperor to against Chi You. Right before Chi You was defeated, he uses his ultimate power rip the space to send his people in.Thousands year was passed, the descendants are still seeking an opportunity to revenge for their ancestor, but lacking of technology is their biggest concern until they found your computer....
As a CSE 308 student, you found there is no better way to kill those invaders by shooting some pixels. You remember your first homework and decide to upgrade it to help you defend your machine. Now fortifying your denfending hardware, keep those monster away from your storage!

Fei Lian

Huo Dou

This monster has a dog like apperence, its body is surrounded by green smoke. It is able to eating fire or spit fire.

Kirin

It is a mythical hooved chimerical creature known in Chinese and other East Asian cultures, said to appear with the imminent arrival or passing of a sage or illustrious ruler.[1] It is a good omen thought to occasion prosperity or serenity. It is often depicted with what looks like fire all over its body.

Red Boy

Red Boy is a character featured in the novel Journey to the West. He is the son of Princess Iron Fan and the Bull Demon King. Through the orders of his father, Red Boy was told to be the protector of the Fiery Mountains due to his supreme abilities when it came to fire.
